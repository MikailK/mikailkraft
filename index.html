About Me

I'm a rising senior at the University of Colorado at Boulder pursuing my major in Spanish accompanied by my minors in Italian and Linguistics. I'm also approaching my fourth summer as a research assistant at NIST, where my work takes form in 3D Printing.

Living in Miami, Florida for nearly half my life has played a big role in my life. Being constantly exposed to the Cuban-American community I was lucky to call home enriches my learning experience indescribably. The more I learn, the more I uncover and understand my own childhood.
